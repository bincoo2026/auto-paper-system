 计算 $\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi }{2}}{x\cos xdx}$.
 解析：原式$=\left. x\sin x \right|_{0}^{\frac{\pi }{2}}-\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi }{2}}{\sin xdx=\frac{\pi }{2}}+\left. \cos x \right|_{0}^{\frac{\pi }{2}}=\frac{\pi }{2}-1$.

计算 $\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi }{2}}{2x\cos x}dx$.
解析：原式$\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi }{2}}{2x\cos x}dx=\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi }{2}}{2x}d\sin x=\left. 2x\sin x \right|_{0}^{\frac{\pi }{2}}-2\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi }{2}}{\sin x}dx=\pi -2$
