计算极限：$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\tan x - \sin x}{x^3}$  
答案：$\frac{1}{2}$  
解析：$$  
\begin{aligned}  
\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\tan x - \sin x}{x^3}  
&= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x (1 - \cos x)}{x^3 \cos x} \\  
&= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{x \cdot \frac{1}{2}x^2}{x^3} = \frac{1}{2}  
\end{aligned}  
$$

计算极限：$\lim_{x \to \infty} \left(1 + \frac{2}{x}\right)^{3x}$  
答案：$e^6$  
解析：令 $t = \frac{x}{2}$，则原式 $= \lim_{t \to \infty} \left(1 + \frac{1}{t}\right)^{6t} = e^6$。
